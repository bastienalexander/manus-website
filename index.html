From markets across Europe, manus seeks out objects with history, craft, and presence - handpicked pieces that give interiors depth and personality.

handpicked objects

Across European markets, ateliers and private collections, manus searches for objects that carry a story. Pieces with character, craft and a presence that gives an interior depth.

Not what is trending, but what endures. manus seeks objects that will speak just as clearly in ten years as they do today.

handmade objects

Every handmade object is one of a kind. manus works with craftspeople and artists who know their materials and take their time — for pieces you will not find just anywhere.

Curators

manus is a personal curator of art objects, vintage finds and handmade work. Each piece is carefully selected for its character, craft and lasting presence.

Sourcing

From Parisian flea markets to Portuguese potters — manus travels across Europe in search of objects with a history and an honest story behind the maker.

Composing interiors

manus does not decorate, but composes. Objects are brought together with intention — so that an interior deepens with time rather than simply ages.

For whom

For private clients, interior designers and developers looking for pieces that make a space feel personal — not styled, but lived with.

curated objects

Every object in the manus collection is personally selected. No bulk buying, no trends — only pieces that manus believes truly belong somewhere.

From a worn wooden stool to a carefully thrown ceramic vase — it is about the feeling an object brings to the space around it.

Not what is trending, but what endures. manus seeks objects that will speak just as clearly in ten years as they do today.
