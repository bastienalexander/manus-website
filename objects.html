Objects

Each object is chosen by hand — for its history, craft and quiet presence.

The manus collection is not a catalogue. It is an ongoing edit — shaped by what we find on our travels across Europe, in studios, at markets and through private collections.

Every piece is selected because it belongs somewhere. Because it carries something. A worn surface, an honest material, a shape that has endured.

We work with a small number of makers and sources we trust. Nothing is produced at scale. Everything is available in limited numbers, and some pieces exist only once.

If you are looking for something specific — a material, a period, a scale — get in touch. We are happy to search with intention.
